Frequently Asked Questions

How can I collaborate with Fara's Kitchen?

I'm always open to collaborations with brands, fellow food bloggers, and content creators. Please use the contact form above to reach out with your proposal, and I'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Can I request a specific recipe?

I love hearing from readers about what recipes they'd like to see. Use the contact form to send your recipe request, and I'll do my best to create and share it on the blog.

Do you offer cooking classes or workshops?

Yes, I occasionally offer virtual cooking classes and in-person workshops in the Mumbai area. Sign up for my newsletter to be notified about upcoming classes and events.

Can I use your recipes on my website or blog?

While I appreciate your interest in my recipes, they are protected by copyright. You may share a link to my recipe on your site, but please do not republish the full recipe without permission. Contact me if you'd like to discuss licensing options.

How can I support Fara's Kitchen?

The best way to support my work is to share my recipes with friends and family, follow me on social media, and leave comments on recipes you've tried. Your engagement helps the blog grow and reach more food enthusiasts!
